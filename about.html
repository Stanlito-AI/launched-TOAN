Registration

TOAN Engineering and Services Limited is a Company registered in Nigeria with CAC and
RC N0: 1566513. We share the Federal Government Vision to develop Nigeria with a view to

Vision

Our Vision is to Grow to become the leader in construction, Agro and Agro Allied products
and services, Human Capacity Development and Technical Training in Nigeria and Africa
at large.

Our Company management goals are forthright: on-time delivery, state-of-the-art materials and
